How to get started with Member Areas

Get started
  1. Create a website with Squarespace, and decide on a name for your member area.
  2. Add member-only content, from classes to newsletters, guides to videos.
  3. Costumize your member area landing and sign-up page with details about what you offer.
  4. Connect your prefferd processor so you can start receiving fees.
  5. Add a Member Areas subscription to your existing Squarespace website plan.
